Artists

Alexander Galloway

Alexander R. Galloway is a writer and computer programer working on issues in philosophy, technology, and theories of mediation. He is author of several books on digital media and critical theory

Martin Wattenberg

Martin Wattenberg is a professor at Harvard and also a part-time member of Google Research's PAIR (People + AI Research) initiative. His work currently focuses on making AI technology broadly accessible and reflective of human values. At Google, his team has also created end-user visualizations for products such as Search, YouTube, and Google Analytics.

Mark Tribe

Mark Tribe is an artist and educator. His work includes painting, photography, video, installation, performance and new media, in various combinations. His recent landscape pictures respond to the difficulties of climate change, particularly as they relate to the wild forests of North America.
